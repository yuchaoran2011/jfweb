A record of media interview with Ellen Pinegar

Source: Thought Catalog

A journalist from Thought Catalog reached out to Ellen Pinegar, a girl who says she was very close with Tracy Nelson.

Ellen Pinegar said she’s known Tracy since they were kids. Their moms are close friends and they both grew up in the Habitat for Humanity neighborhood in northwest Springfield.

In October 2014, Tracy began messaging her through a secret Facebook page under the name Emma Rose.

“Hi Ellen its Tracy,” the first post read. “This is my personal account my mom is still overprotective so she doesn’t know about this account.”

Then Tracy began writing about her life:

“I’m 17. Ken my boyfriend is 24, we met on a Christian Singles site, we love each other.”

“He plans on popping the question sometime in 2016, and we want the wedding date to be in December of 2017. If all goes good we want a snow wedding in a gazebo with red and white roses.”

“We are very serious we say we aren’t dating we are courting. Cuz we are both Catholic Christians.”
