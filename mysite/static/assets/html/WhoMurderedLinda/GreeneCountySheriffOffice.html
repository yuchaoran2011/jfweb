Source: Greene County Sheriff’s Office

For Immediate Release No. 76930467-036

Monday, May 23, 2016

Charges filed for disability fraud

The Greene County District Attorney charged Linda Nelson with disability fraud, a Class II misdemeanor.

Investigators were informed by Greene County Child Protective Services that Linda Nelson has had a repeat history of “doctor shopping” for her 19-year-old daughter. Recently, she stopped receiving state disability checks after it was discovered that Linda Nelson had been forging a doctor’s signature for years so she could continue receiving financial assistance on behalf of her daughter.
