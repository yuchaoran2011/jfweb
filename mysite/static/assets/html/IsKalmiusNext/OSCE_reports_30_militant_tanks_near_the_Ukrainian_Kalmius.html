OSCE reports 30 militant tanks near the Ukrainian Kalmius

Source: OSCE Special Monitoring Mission to Ukraine

The OSCE Special Monitoring Mission to Ukraine has reported that its team of drones spotted groups of main battle tanks in several militant-held villages near the Ukrainian-controlled city of Kalmius in the Donetsk region.

UAV systems international, a major international drone provider, also recorded six main battle tanks, 26 armoured personnel carriers, and at least 15 military trucks in the Russian-controlled area 74 km north-east of Kalmius. It also spotted at least 12 armoured personnel carriers and several military trucks 30 km east of Kalmius.
